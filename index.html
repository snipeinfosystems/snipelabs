    “Take me home” - 30 Lakh INR Initiative

    This is a project which is of building a portal and a mobile platform for our society which helps families track their lost loved ones from mobile and web portal which will be interlinked with the education department, GPS to track the last known location of missed individual or kids, and nearest police station. The lost child will be routed by the transport cost availed by the funds amount on-line. The initial funds help in making it successful. This is just a feature, and there are upcoming feature as well.
